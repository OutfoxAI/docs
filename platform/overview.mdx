## Why this matters

Most teams split their work across:

- inspiration databases
- campaign dashboards
- analytics exports
- briefs and planning docs

Outfox MCP compresses that into one conversational loop.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the pattern">
    Use inspiration tools to surface ads worth learning from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Translate it into strategy">
    Convert the best example into a brief tailored to your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pressure-test it with performance context">
    Bring Meta analytics into the same thread so the next decision is grounded
    in real performance signals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
