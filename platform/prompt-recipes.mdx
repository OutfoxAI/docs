## Research prompts

```text
Search inspiration for top-performing DTC skincare ads on Instagram. Keep the results concise and explain why each one is worth saving.
```

```text
Find five live supplement ads with strong hooks and tell me which hook patterns repeat.
```

## Brief prompts

```text
Use the best inspiration post in that list and generate a brief for my brand. Make it premium, visual, and direct-response.
```

```text
Generate three variations of that brief: one safe, one bold, and one founder-led.
```

## Analytics prompts

```text
Show me the top-performing creatives in this ad account for the last 14 days and summarize the patterns in plain English.
```

```text
Compare the strongest campaign and weakest campaign in this account and tell me what the loser should steal from the winner.
```

## Combined prompts

```text
Search inspiration for ads that match the best-performing creative patterns in this account, then generate a new brief for my brand.
```

```text
Take the strongest inspiration pattern and strongest Meta performer, then propose three new testable angles with an explanation for each.
```

## How to get better answers

- Ask the model to cite the exact tools or posts it relied on
- Ask for a ranking, not just a dump of results
- Ask for tradeoffs between ideas
- Ask for the next best action, not just analysis
