---
title: "Example workflows"
description: "Use these workflows to move from inspiration research to creative strategy and analytics review."
---

## Workflow 1: Find a winning pattern and adapt it

**Goal:** move from inspiration search to a brand-specific brief.

```text
Search inspiration for high-converting skincare video ads on Instagram.
```

```text
Use the best post you found and generate a brief for my brand.
```

This is the fastest way to move from "show me what works" to "tell me what to
make."

## Workflow 2: Add performance context

**Goal:** connect creative patterns to campaign results.

```text
Show me the top-performing creatives in this ad account for the last 14 days.
```

```text
Compare the top performers to the inspiration patterns you found earlier and tell me what overlaps.
```

This workflow is where the suite gets interesting. You stop treating
inspiration and analytics as separate modes.

## Workflow 3: Brand strategy loop

**Goal:** pressure-test brand direction with both creative and analytics data.

```text
List my brands, then show me the one with the strongest recent creative performance.
```

```text
Generate three new brief angles based on that brand, recent winners, and the best inspiration patterns in the library.
```

## Workflow 4: Team-ready handoff

**Goal:** generate a clean output another marketer can use immediately.

Ask the model to return:

- the inspiration posts it used
- the Meta analytics signals it trusted
- the brief it recommends
- the next three tests to run

<Tip>
  The best results come from asking the model to explain *why* it chose a
  pattern, not just *what* it chose.
</Tip>
